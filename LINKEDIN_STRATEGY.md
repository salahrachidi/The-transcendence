## Introduction: The "Psychology" of Recruitment
Recruiters scan profiles for specific signals. Your goal isn't just to say "I built an app." It's to say "I solve hard problems."

**Key Factors We Are Leveraging:**
1.  **Curiosity (The Hook):** Making them want to click "See More".
2.  **Competence (The Stack):** Showing, not just telling, that you know modern tools (Vault, Docker).
3.  **Communication (The Story):** Engineers who can write are rare. Good writing = Good communication skills.
